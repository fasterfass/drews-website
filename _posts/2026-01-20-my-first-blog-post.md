This blog doesn't really have any limits or bounds to what posts will be about. It's mainly a landing spot for wherever my attention lies at a given time, and is meant to serve as an archieve for how my thoughts change over time. In a world with so much complexity (growing every day) and so many independent actors (doing more things faster and faster all of the time), I'm pretty confident that even with a consistent lens the way I see the world now will not be the way I see it in 5, 10, 20, 50 years. I want a way to capture that perspecive and codify it, so I can truly reflect back on my experiences without them being fully lost to time.

As for the audience, I'm going to write for my future self. I don't mind if nobody sees it or reads it. I'm not planning to publish it for others to consume. If people find it organically great - if it motivates them great - if it helps lead me to a new career path or can be used to show my past experiences great. But that's not the goal. The goal is purely to get in the practice of not forgetting my realizations and lessons learned over time.

## Tips for Writing Posts

1. **Front Matter**: The `---` section at the top defines metadata:
   - `title`: Post title
   - `date`: Publication date (YYYY-MM-DD format)
   - `categories`: Broad topic areas
   - `tags`: Specific tags for filtering
   - `excerpt`: Short summary shown in post lists

2. **File Naming**: Save as `_posts/YYYY-MM-DD-title-slug.md`
   - Example: `_posts/2026-01-20-my-first-post.md`
